## Sovereign Cells (Concept Stage — Architecturally Designed)

**The pitch:** "Don't just build on ACiD. Run your own piece of the chain."

Sovereign Cells are micro-sequencers that builders and AI agents run on their own hardware. State settles back to ACiD L2, which settles to Ethereum L1. This is NOT an L3, and NOT a separate chain — it is local execution with ACiD as the settlement and data-availability layer.

**Why this matters:**
- AI agents making 10,000 micro-decisions per minute can't afford to hit a shared public sequencer for each one. Sovereign Cells let agents run locally and batch-settle.
- Builders avoid RPC centralization by owning their own execution.
- The trust model is transparent: the Cell operates within ACiD's rules, enforces them locally, and settles cryptographically to L2.

**Delivery format:** Go binary, Docker image, `acid cell` CLI. Open source, MIT licensed.

**Status:** Designed, documented, not yet built. Originated from a NoClaw (the founder's AI agent, @nobanksclaw_bot) conversation on 2026-02-24, architected by Claude Opus, documented in CONCEPT_SOVEREIGN_CELLS_AND_NEUTRALIZER.md in the ACiD repo. Four-phase implementation plan exists.

## The Neutralizer (Concept Stage — Architecturally Designed)

**The pitch:** "Your failures have value here."

The Neutralizer is a protocol that lets users bridge worthless or rugged assets from other chains to ACiD. Those assets are permanently burned. In exchange, users earn **pH Credits** — a soulbound (non-transferable) reputation metric.

**What pH Credits unlock:**
- Reduced gas fees on ACiD
- Sovereign Cell access
- Airdrop eligibility
- Governance weight

**Why this works:**
- Turns negative wallet history into narrative ammunition
- Creates a unique top-of-funnel: users with dead positions elsewhere become ACiD-native users
- "Proof of pH" — the more alkaline/institutional your chain history, the more rewards you earn by reclaiming it

**Legal note embedded in concept:** pH Credits are reputation, not a tradeable token. Not a financial product.

**Status:** Designed, documented, not yet built. Same origin as Sovereign Cells (NoClaw concept, Claude Opus refinement, Feb 2026).
