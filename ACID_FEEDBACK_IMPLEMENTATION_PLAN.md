## Approved (7)

### 1. pH Balance Whitepaper
Mathematically model the trade-offs between institutional (Base) and counterculture (Acid) L2 networks. Makes the thesis defensible, not just memeable.
**Effort:** Low | **Priority:** HIGH

### 2. Founder's Story Page
Highlight the collaboration between a creative producer and AI tools (Claude, NoClaw) to build the chain. "Infrastructure with soul" origin story.
**Effort:** Low | **Priority:** HIGH

### 3. Neutralizer Landing Page
Dedicated page explaining how to burn dead tokens and earn pH Credits. This IS the top-of-funnel.
**Effort:** Medium | **Priority:** HIGH

### 4. pH Credit Dashboard
Users visualize their reputation score + benefits (gas discounts, Sovereign Cell access, governance weight).
**Effort:** Medium | **Priority:** MEDIUM

### 5. Dead Token Registry
Dashboard where users find and bridge their specific dead/rugged tokens. Makes the Neutralizer actionable.
**Effort:** Medium | **Priority:** MEDIUM

### 6. Battle-Scarred Soulbound Badges
Gamified certification for users who burn significant dead tokens. Low-effort, high-engagement.
**Effort:** Low | **Priority:** MEDIUM

### 7. Counter-Culture Branding Guide
Defines "acid vs base" ecosystem roles so developers and users understand the positioning visually.
**Effort:** Low | **Priority:** LOW
